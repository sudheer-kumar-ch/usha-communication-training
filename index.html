Usha Sarda

Trainer | Motivational Speaker | Life Coach | Author

Usha Sarda is an experienced and internationally certified trainer, life coach, NLP Master Practitioner, Published Author, Motivational Speaker with a robust background in Finance coupled with over a decade of extensive teaching expertise. She also published book “Lost and Found”.

Presently, she channels her wealth of knowledge into honing diverse soft skills such as communication, motivation, empathy, and NLP. Renowned for her innovative approach, Usha’s teaching methodology is characterized by dynamic activities and stimulating discussions, fostering an immersive learning environment.

She has conducted more than 100 workshops. Among her most acclaimed programs are “Enhance your Communication Skills”, “NLP Courses” & “Moving Ahead with Movies,” a groundbreaking initiative that harnesses the power of visual storytelling to facilitate skill acquisition.

She is mainly known for making complex subjects simple and easy to understand, easily relatable, activity, and visual based learning.
